plus2a.rom:

This ROM shipped with the UK Spectrum +2A (PCB Revision Z70833). It is also compatible with the +2B or Plus 3 although you may prefer to use their native ROMs. It is version 4.1 which contains minor bugfixes compared with v4.0 which shipped with the Plus 3.

plus2aesp.rom:

Spahish localisation of the Plus 2A v4.1 ROM.
