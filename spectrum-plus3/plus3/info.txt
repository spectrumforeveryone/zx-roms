Sinclair ZX Spectrum +3 ROM (Original 4.0)

===

Copyright 1987 Amstrad Plc

===

Compatible with:
 - ZX Spectrum +2A / +2B
 - ZX Spectrum +3A / +3B

===

This ROM is the original one that shipped with the Spectrum +3 and consists of 4 x 16K ROMs - one each for 48K BASIC and the 128K Editor and two for +3DOS.

It is compatible with the +2A and +2B although they use a slightly newer revision and you would be better off using them.
